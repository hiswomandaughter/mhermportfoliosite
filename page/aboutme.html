About Me:

I am a second-year student at Michigan State University. My major: Arts and Humanities and Professional Writing with a specialization in Museum Studies. Currently, I work in the MSU Main Library, and my interests include exhibit design and creating environments where people are able to see themselves in history, science, and art in order to not only internalize the material more completely, but to also allow them the chance to appreciate the world around them.

My background in the RCAH gives me the experience in leading and engaging in discussions surrounding problems of cultural ostracization in places such as museums, as well as fostering a passion for the skills I'm interested in: design. However, with my Professional Writing major, I've been able to use my writing skills to voice these skills for the job market.
